Let's start exploring the KDA ALL OUT collection to better understand them.

Officially debuting for the first time in 2018, the K / DA skin group has attracted a lot of attention from LoL fans. And to serve the community's love, Riot Games has specially introduced the Prestige version for all members of this skin group. And this time to introduce the epic comeback of this virtual band, the publisher of League of Legends decided to introduce a new line of skins called All Out. Accordingly, this group of costumes includes the presence of Akali, Evelynn, Ahri, Kai'Sa. This new All Out outfit group will have white, blue and similar colors to the shining diamond. Accordingly, the members look like real music stars.
